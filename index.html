Product Families

Two families. One intelligence.

Each family shares a core analytical engine. The products within a family reinforce each other — same intelligence, applied at different scales.

Strata
Understand the market. Position to win.

Every market has a landscape — who's hiring and why, which competitors are gaining ground, where the real openings are. Strata reads that landscape with precision and translates it into a clear position: here is where you fit, here is how to present yourself, here is what to decide. At any scale.

Strata Work Pre-alpha
Compete for the right roles — and win them.

Strata Work does the research most candidates skip. For every opportunity you pursue, it builds a company brief — differentiators, recent news, leadership backgrounds, hiring signals — and a targeted prep package: customized resume and cover letter, likely interview questions, and how to frame your experience to land. When gaps exist, it surfaces them and builds a roadmap to close them: training, projects, certifications. When you're ready to be found, it helps you build the articles and social presence that make your expertise visible to the companies you're targeting. You focus on the opportunities worth winning. Agents handle everything else.

Strata Strategy In development
Intelligence that remembers. Decisions that compound.

Every CI tool on the market collects signals and sends alerts. None of them remember what you decided, why you decided it, or what happened next. Strata Strategy does. Each strategic decision is logged with its context, the signals that triggered it, and the outcome — so the system learns what works for your specific organization and market. By month six it recognizes patterns. By month twelve, your institutional intelligence is irreplaceable: a queryable record of every call your team made and why, getting sharper with every decision. Not a dashboard. A compounding strategic advantage.

Hearth
Own it. Know it. Pass it on.

Households generate an enormous amount of information — what you own, who services it, when it was last maintained, what it cost, who needs it next. Nobody built a good place to put all of it. Hearth manages the full lifecycle of ownership: from what comes into your home, to how it's cared for, to the moment it finds its next owner — with its story intact.

Hearth Keep In development
Buy less. Gift better. Give things a second life.

A trusted network for intentional ownership. Before anything is purchased, Hearth Keep asks: does someone in your trusted circle already have this? Rehoming within your network comes first — matched by life stage, needs, and what people actually want. When a purchase makes sense, durability guidance steers you toward things that last, with explicit reasoning. Group gifting and pooling for life moments: graduation, first home, new baby, downsizing. When something moves on, the care record from Hearth Tend travels with it — so the next owner knows exactly what they're getting.

Design Philosophy

How we build. Why it matters.

Principles that inform every architectural decision — from the individual dashboard to the enterprise briefing room.

01

AI-assisted, never AI-replaced

High-stakes decisions always have a human gate. Agents prepare — they scrape, score, synthesize, and recommend. Humans decide. This isn't a limitation; it's the architecture. Systems that remove human judgment in consequential domains create liability, not leverage.

02

Intelligence that compounds

Every product is built on a persistent memory architecture — Living Memory for user context and preferences, Alexandria for structured knowledge that accumulates across sessions. Month one is good. Month six is better. Month twelve is irreplaceable. The system doesn't reset when you close the tab.

03

Privacy-first by design

Lexenne products handle personal and proprietary data — career history, home details, spending patterns, competitive strategy. We treat that as a privilege. Local inference where possible. No data resold. Transparent about what we store and why.

04

Enterprise-grade from the start

Governed multi-agent orchestration, cost-tier routing, decision traceability, and human-gated output — not stitched together after the fact. The engineering discipline behind Lexenne comes from production AI deployments at enterprise scale. Confidence in a system comes from the architecture, not the demo.

Engineering Foundation

Production-grade AI.
Built to compound.

Lexenne products run on governed multi-agent orchestration — purpose-built, not assembled from off-the-shelf components. Each specialist agent operates within defined scope, cost bounds, and observability constraints. Consequential outputs require human confirmation before any action is taken.

Two persistent memory systems underlie every product: Living Memory maintains user context, preferences, and workspace state across sessions. Alexandria captures structured knowledge — decisions, patterns, rationale — that compounds rather than evaporates. The longer the system runs, the more precisely it models what matters.

The orchestration layer manages each task's full lifecycle: decomposition, specialist routing, output consolidation, and quality gate — before any result surfaces to the user. Decisions carry traces. Every output has an auditable path from question to answer that can be reviewed and challenged. That traceability is what makes AI-assisted work trustworthy in high-stakes domains.
